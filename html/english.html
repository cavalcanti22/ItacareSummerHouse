Itacaré

City Tips

Itacaré has a charming landscape, formed by a sequence of beaches, whose nature is still practically untouched by man, with hills covered by forests and many coconut trees. It is the first beach of the "Cacao Coast", on the coast of Bahia, south of Salvador. Nestled between the sea and the Atlantic Rainforest, Itacaré is the ideal option for those who like a beach allied with nightlife with a lot of excitement, as there are always concerts and parties, as well as bars and nightclubs that run all year round.

Itacaré is also a great destination for ecotourism enthusiasts and extreme sports enthusiasts. There is an Environmental Preservation Area, where you can go rafting, mountain biking, canopy tours, trails and abseiling - or just enjoy the exuberance of the Atlantic Forest with its rivers and waterfalls.

There are 14 main beaches in Itacaré, each with very particular characteristics. Those close to the city have good tourist infrastructure, with restaurants and inns. Already the most distant are almost deserted, being accessible only by trails. Former refuge of colonels of cacao, the village of Itacaré preserves the architecture of the past. Many mansions have been renovated and now house restaurants, bars, cafes, nightclubs and shops.

The beaches of Itacaré attract many surfers, due to the strong waves, considered the best in Bahia, and warm waters all year round, including in winter.

    How To Get There?

    Daily arrivals from TAM, GOL, AZUL and AVIANCA arriving from São Paulo, Brasília, Belo Horizonte and Salvador arrive at Ilhéus (IOS) - at 72 km - from Itacaré.

  • By Land

    Private Vehicle

    The BR-101 is the main access for those who come from the southern region of the country. For those coming from the north, the best access is by BA-001.

    For those who come from Salvador, the best option is to take the ferry boat to Bom Despacho, on the Island of Itaparica, and follow the BA-001. Departing from the south, such as Porto Seguro, Arraial D'Ajuda or Trancoso, it is best to take the BR-367 to Eunápolis, then follow the BR-101 towards Itabuna. At the junction with the BA-263, turn right onto Ilhéus. In Ilhéus take the BA-001, heading north / Itacaré beaches.
